EPSILON⁰

Drawing inspiration from the concept of Epsilon⁰, the permittivity of free space in physics, our car's name, Epsilon, pays homage to the fundamental properties of space. Epsilon Zero represents the measure of a vacuum's ability to support electric fields, serving as a benchmark for the interaction of charged particles. By adopting the name Epsilon, we embody the spirit of pushing boundaries and breaking barriers in our pursuit of speed and performance. Just as Epsilon Zero allows for the uninhibited propagation of electric fields, our car, Epsilon, represents our commitment to unleashing the full potential of engineering and technology, propelling us to new heights on the racetrack. With sleek design, advanced aerodynamics, and cutting-edge innovations, Epsilon exemplifies our dedication to harnessing the power of physics and technology to create a winning formula.

TEAM CONCEPT

At Delta 7, our team concept centers around the fusion of innovation, teamwork, and a deep appreciation for the Formula 1 legacy. Inspired by the renowned racing teams, we aim to create a visually captivating car that excels in speed and aerodynamics. By incorporating cutting-edge technology, such as advanced CFD simulations and lightweight materials, we push the boundaries of performance. Through collaborative teamwork, we leverage our diverse skills to overcome challenges and continuously strive for excellence. With a relentless pursuit of innovation, Delta 7 aims to leave a lasting impact in the F1 in Schools competition and inspire future generations of engineers and designers.
