F1 in schools

In F1 in Schools, teams of students aged 9 to 19 deploy CAD/CAM software to collaborate, design, analyze, manufacture, test, and then race miniature compressed air powered cars made from the F1 model block. Teams must also raise sponsorship and manage budgets to fund research, travel and accommodation.
